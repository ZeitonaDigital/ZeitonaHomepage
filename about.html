About Zeitona

Zeitona is a pioneer in Construction 5.0, transforming how the industry builds, manages, and delivers projects through integrated digital technologies.

Founded by engineers and innovators who understand both construction and code, we're committed to solving the industry's greatest challenges: inefficiency, lack of transparency, and contractual disputes.

Our mission is to create a human-centric, sustainable, and digitally sovereign construction ecosystem where every stakeholder — architects, engineers, suppliers, contractors, and regulators — operates from a single verifiable source of truth. We deliver full compliance with regulatory standards, complete transparency across the supply chain, comprehensive auditability with full audit trails, and verified trust through secure, blockchain-backed systems.
